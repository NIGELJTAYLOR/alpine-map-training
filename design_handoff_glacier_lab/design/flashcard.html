Question
What does tightening contour spacing toward a summit tell you about the aspect's slope angle?
Answer
That aspect is the steepest line from the summit. The closer the rings compress, the more vertical metres are packed into the same horizontal distance — the steeper the descent on that side. In poor visibility, this is your most reliable cue for picking a descent before you can see snow.
