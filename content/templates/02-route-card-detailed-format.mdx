**Used in:** D9.2 for high-detail planning of complex legs; D9.4 escape-route exercise. One sheet per leg.

**How to use:** print one sheet per leg of the tour. The format gives more space for terrain notes and contingency thinking on legs that need it.

```
Tour name: ___________________________________________________
Date: __________     Leg ___ of ___     Variation: _____ ° E / W

Start point: ___________________________________   Altitude: ________ m
End point:   ___________________________________   Altitude: ________ m

Bearings:    Grid ________°       Magnetic ________°
Distance:    ________ m           Estimated time: ________ min
Altimeter trigger height: ________ m

Key terrain features (in order along the leg):
1. ________________________________________________________________
2. ________________________________________________________________
3. ________________________________________________________________
4. ________________________________________________________________

Avalanche assessment:
Slope angle: ________°    Aspect: __________    Elevation band: __________
Terrain traps along the leg: __________________________________________
Runout / overhead exposure: ___________________________________________

Decision point criteria (three-component standard):
Trigger: ______________________________________________________________
Action:  ______________________________________________________________
Location: ____________________________________________________________

Escape route:
Description: __________________________________________________________
Conditions where unavailable: _________________________________________
Alternative if escape route fails: ___________________________________

Notes: ________________________________________________________________
______________________________________________________________________
```
