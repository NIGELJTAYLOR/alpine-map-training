**Used in:** D9.4 across the full route card. Worksheet for stress-testing escape routes before transferring them to the route card.

**How to use:** for each leg, identify at least one escape route. Stress-test by asking when the escape would not work. Flag any leg with no viable escape as a committing leg requiring tighter entry criteria or a route revision.

```
A "committing leg" is one with no viable escape route. The IMS / EMS-relevant response:
1. Recognise the commitment explicitly on the route card.
2. Tighten the entry criteria for the leg (e.g. higher weather standard).
3. Reconsider the route choice if multiple legs are committing.

Question to ask: if I had to abandon the tour at the start of this leg,
where would I go and how would I get there?

If there is no good answer, the leg is committing.

Number of committing legs in this tour: ______
If three or more, consider revising the route.
```
