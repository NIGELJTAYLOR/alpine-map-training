**Used in:** D9.2 onwards, plus referenced throughout D8 (the route card is the foundation of the whiteout sequence). One sheet per tour day.

**How to use:** print one sheet per tour. Each row is a field; each column is a leg. Six legs is typical for a full-day tour; if more or fewer, leave columns blank or add an extra sheet.
