**What to point out:** the lines cross at a single point; the angle of crossing is sharpest when the two features are ~90 degrees apart. The procedure works only when at least two map-identifiable features are visible on the ground, which is why poor-visibility relocation uses the aspect-of-slope technique (D7.2) instead.
