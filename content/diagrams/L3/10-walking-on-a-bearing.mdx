**What to point out:** the candidate's body tracks the eyes, so the eyes go on a feature, not on the compass. Each leg ends at a distinctive, stable feature; the bearing is reset before the next leg. In featureless terrain the aiming point becomes a partner sent forward (the basis of D8.2 pole-and-cord).
