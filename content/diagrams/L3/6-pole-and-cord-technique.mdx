**What to point out:** the lead navigator holds one end of the cord and the pole; the scout walks forward on the bearing paying out the cord; the cord on the snow gives a physical confirmation of direction; if the scout finds a hazard (drop, cornice margin), the cord allows the lead navigator to react.
