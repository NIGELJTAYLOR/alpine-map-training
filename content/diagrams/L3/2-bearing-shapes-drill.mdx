**What to point out:** each shape closes back to the starting point if compass and pacing are accurate; the closure error (gap between start and end) is the cumulative effect of every bearing inaccuracy and every pacing inconsistency over the legs; the IMS / EMS standard is less than 5 paces closure on a 100-pace square.
