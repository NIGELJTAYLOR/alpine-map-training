**What to point out:** the approach view of a cliff band from below. The face has extremely tight contour spacing where the wall rises steeply; the apron and approach slope below the wall has noticeably wider contour spacing. Note that this photo is taken from mid-air on a moving cable car, so the camera position is approximate. Matches contour snippet 5. The cue is the abrupt transition from wide-spaced contours (approach slope) to densely-packed contours (cliff band).
