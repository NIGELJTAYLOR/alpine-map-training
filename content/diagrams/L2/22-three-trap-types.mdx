**What to point out:** all three are recognisable from the contour pattern alone. The narrow gully shows tight V's with rock symbology on the walls; the bowl shows concentric contours converging on a constricted mouth; the slope above a cliff shows even spacing tightening into a band of merged contours with rock symbology. Reading these from the map gives you the chance to plan around them.
