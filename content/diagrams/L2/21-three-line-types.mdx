**What to point out:** the direct line is shortest but holds the slope's full gradient throughout; the contour-following line is the gentlest but adds the most horizontal distance; the zig-zag splits the climb across traverses inside the comfortable skinning band, with kick turns placed where the ground is locally gentlest. Most touring lines combine all three.
