**What to point out:** the IGN 1:50,000 series uses 10 m contour intervals (same as Swisstopo 1:25,000), brown contour lines, blue water, green forest, and a distinctive pink hatching for built-up areas and lift infrastructure. Compared with the Swisstopo 1:25,000 sheet at L1.11, this sheet shows the same kind of features at half the scale: each centimetre on the page covers 500 m on the ground instead of 250 m. A vector PDF version is available at `/diagrams/L1/12-ign-1-50000-trois-vallees.pdf` for unlimited-resolution zoom. Map data © IGN, served via Géoportail (geoportail.gouv.fr).
