**What to point out:** the same scene appears twice in different views. The map view uses symbols (stream as a blue line, bridge as a black crossing, buildings as small black rectangles) and contour spacing to encode shape; the ground view shows what those map clues translate to at eye level. Close contours on the east of the map become a visibly steeper slope on the right of the side profile.
