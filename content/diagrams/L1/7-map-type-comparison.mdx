**What to point out:** the road map shows roads, town blobs and distances but no terrain shape; the satellite image shows real surfaces (trees, rock, snow, water) but is hard to read quickly for slope and route; the topographic map combines symbols, contours and labels so the shape of the land, the water and the features can be read at a glance. For mountain navigation, contours decide the line.
