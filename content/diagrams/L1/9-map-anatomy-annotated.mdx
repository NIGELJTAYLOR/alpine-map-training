**What to point out:** title and series sit in the top strip; the north arrow is a small block, often near a corner of the map area; grid lines run across the sheet with numbers around the edges; the legend explains every symbol and colour; the scale bar plus statement tells you how map distance maps to ground distance. The same five elements appear on every alpine sheet in roughly the same places.
