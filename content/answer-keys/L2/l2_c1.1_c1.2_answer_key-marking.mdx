If a learner struggles with the 30 degree rule, return to the worked example and walk through the maths once together: rise over run, atan, and the practical rule of thumb. The maths is not memorised in the assessment; the rule of thumb is.

If a learner forgets to check the contour interval and applies the 3 mm rule to a 1:50,000 map or a 25 m interval Swisstopo sheet, treat that as the most important error to correct. It is the trap that catches most candidates.
