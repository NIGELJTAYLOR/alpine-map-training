### Exercise 2 — Why rotate?
A strong answer should explain that rotating the map helps the directions on the paper match the directions of the terrain, which reduces mental effort and makes route understanding easier.

### Exercise 3 — Tabletop practice
Answers vary. The learner should show that some positions of the paper make interpretation easier than others.

### Exercise 4 — Feature matching
Answers vary. Check that the learner understands they are matching the direction of the map feature to the equivalent feature in the real world.
