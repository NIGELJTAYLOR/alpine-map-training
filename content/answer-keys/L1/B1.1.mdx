### Exercise 1 — Identify the map type
- A. Road map
- B. Satellite image
- C. Topographic map

### Exercise 2 — Why use a topographic map?
Accept any three valid points, such as:

- It shows the shape of the land.
- It shows steep and gentle ground.
- It helps with route choice in mountain terrain.
- It combines natural and man-made features.
- It is designed for navigation rather than just travel between settlements.

### Exercise 3 — Short comparison task
Model answers:

| View type | Best for | Weakness |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Road map | Roads, towns, route planning by road | Poor for terrain shape |
| Satellite image | Real-world surface appearance | Harder to judge terrain structure quickly |
| Topographic map | Navigation and terrain understanding | Requires learning symbols and contours |

### Exercise 4 — Short written response
A strong answer should mention most of the following:

- A topographic map shows the shape of the land.
- It helps the learner understand high and low ground.
- It shows important natural and man-made features together.
- It is better for judging route practicality in mountain areas.
- It supports navigation decisions more effectively than a simple road map.
