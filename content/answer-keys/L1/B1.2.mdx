### Exercise 1 — Simple translation
A strong answer should include:

- A stream in the lowest part of the ground.
- A bridge crossing the stream.
- Buildings grouped on the west side.
- Steeper rising ground to the east.
- Gentler or flatter ground around the buildings to the west.

### Exercise 2 — “If I stood here...”
A strong answer should include ideas such as:

- Water below or beside the bridge.
- Buildings behind or to one side on flatter ground.
- The slope rising more steeply to the east.
- The valley floor or lower ground around the stream.

### Exercise 3 — Feature matching
- Blue line at the lowest point = B. Watercourse
- Cluster of black rectangles = C. Buildings
- Close brown contour lines = A. Rising slope

### Exercise 4 — Sketch task
This is self-assessed, but a good sketch should show:

- Stream at the lowest point.
- Bridge crossing the stream.
- Buildings on the west side.
- Steeper slope to the east.
- Relative ground shape shown from side view rather than from above.
