### Exercise 1 — Fast conversions

#### 1:25,000 scale
1. 1 cm = 250 m
2. 2 cm = 500 m
3. 4 cm = 1 km
4. 6 cm = 1.5 km

#### 1:50,000 scale
5. 1 cm = 500 m
6. 2 cm = 1 km
7. 4 cm = 2 km
8. 6 cm = 3 km

### Exercise 2 — Mixed drill

| Scale | Map distance | Ground distance |
|-------|--------------|-----------------|
| 1:25,000 | 5 cm | 1.25 km |
| 1:50,000 | 5 cm | 2.5 km |
| 1:25,000 | 10 cm | 2.5 km |
| 1:50,000 | 10 cm | 5 km |
| 1:25,000 | 12 cm | 3 km |
| 1:50,000 | 12 cm | 6 km |

### Exercise 3 — Which is larger?
1. 4 cm represents the greater ground distance on a 1:50,000 map.
2. The route on the 1:50,000 map is longer on the ground.
3. Because the same map measurement means different real distances on different scales.
