If the learner struggles, check whether the difficulty is mainly:

- recognising symbols,
- understanding that water lies in low ground,
- understanding that close contours mean steep ground,
- or converting map clues into a side-on mental picture.

If the last point is the main issue, keep using tiny map scenes and ask for short verbal descriptions before asking for sketches.
