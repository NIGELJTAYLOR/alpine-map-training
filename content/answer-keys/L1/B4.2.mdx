### Exercise 1 — Straight route conversions

| Scale | Map measurement | Ground distance |
|-------|-----------------|-----------------|
| 1:25,000 | 3 cm | 750 m |
| 1:25,000 | 8 cm | 2 km |
| 1:50,000 | 4 cm | 2 km |
| 1:50,000 | 7 cm | 3.5 km |

### Exercise 2 — Printed extract practice
Answers depend on the learner’s chosen extract. Check that:

- the correct scale has been identified,
- the learner has measured sensibly,
- and the conversion matches the scale used.

### Exercise 3 — Curved route practice
Again, answers vary. A strong answer should show that the learner understands why curved routes are harder to measure and why string or paper helps follow bends.

### Exercise 4 — Print-check question
A strong answer should mention that if a PDF is printed at the wrong size, the original scale may no longer be accurate, so measurements may be wrong unless checked against the scale bar.
