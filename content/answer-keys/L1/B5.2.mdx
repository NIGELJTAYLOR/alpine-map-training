### Exercise 2 — Read the grid reference
1. 2741 means the square right of easting 27 and above northing 41.
2. 5633 means the square right of easting 56 and above northing 33.
3. 1248 means the square right of easting 12 and above northing 48.

### Exercise 3 — Spot the mistake
The learner has reversed the order and said the northing first instead of the easting first.

Correct answer: 3752.

### Exercise 4 — Practice on a printed extract
Answers vary. Check that the learner:

- has chosen real features,
- has identified the correct square,
- and has given eastings before northings.
