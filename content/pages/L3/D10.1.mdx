### Learning aim

By the end of this page, you should have a clear, honest picture of which Level 3 skills are fluent, which are slow, and which are unreliable. The quiz is not graded for the workbook record; it is graded for your own progression decision.

### How to use this page

The quiz has fifteen short questions, drawn from across Sections D1 to D9. Work through them in order at a comfortable pace.

After you finish, your trainer will mark with you. Use the error log at the end of the page to record:

- which questions you got wrong,
- which questions you got right but slowly,
- which Level 3 page each weak question relates to,
- and what you will do about it before D10.2.

### Why this matters for IMS

D10.1 is the first sustained mixed-skills check of Level 3 (mirroring the role of C7.1 in Level 2). It is not the IMS / EMS assessment, but it is the first time the candidate has to switch fluently between Level 3 skills under light time pressure, which is what the assessor will require in the field. The error log is the page's most useful artefact: it identifies exactly which earlier pages need a second pass before D10.2.
