### Learning aim

By the end of this page, you should have a clear, honest picture of which Level 2 skills are fluent, which are slow, and which are unreliable. The quiz is not graded for the workbook record; it is graded for your own progression decision.

### How to use this page

The quiz has fifteen short questions, drawn from across Sections C1 to C6. Work through them in order at a comfortable pace.

After you finish, your trainer will mark with you. Use the error log at the end of the page to record:

- which questions you got wrong,
- which questions you got right but slowly,
- which Level 2 page each weak question relates to,
- and what you will do about it before C7.2.

Working materials: the Section C7.1 map extract (provided by your trainer; 1:25,000, 10 m contours) plus the Section C7.1 snippet sheet for the matching question.

### Why this matters for IMS

C7.1 is the first sustained mixed-skills check in the workbook. It is not the IMS / EMS assessment, but it is the first time the learner has to switch fluently between skills under light time pressure, which is what the assessor will require in the field. The error log is the page's most useful artefact: it identifies exactly which earlier pages need a second pass before Level 3.
