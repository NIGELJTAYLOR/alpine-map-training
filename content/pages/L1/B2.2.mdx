### Learning aim
By the end of this page, the learner should be able to locate key map elements quickly and confidently on any topographic map.

### Why speed matters
In real navigation, you will often need to:

- Confirm the scale quickly.  
- Check a symbol in the legend.  
- Read a grid reference near the edge.  
- Glance at the north arrow.

The less time you spend hunting for these basics, the more attention you can give to route choice, hazards and the group.

### Exercise 1 — Timed anatomy drill

You will need a stopwatch or phone timer.

Step 1: On your chosen map, time how long it takes to find and touch each of the following in order:

1. Title or map name.  
2. Scale statement.  
3. Scale bar.  
4. Legend.  
5. North arrow.  
6. A grid line.  
7. A grid number.
