### Learning aim
By the end of this page, the learner should be able to convert common map measurements quickly and confidently without having to work everything out from scratch each time.

### Quick rules to memorise
Use these fluency rules:

- **1:25,000** → 1 cm = 250 m
- **1:50,000** → 1 cm = 500 m

If these become automatic, route planning becomes much easier.
